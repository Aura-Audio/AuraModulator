# Product Marketing Plan: AuraModulator

## 1. Executive Summary
**AuraModulator** is an extreme, browser-based Audio DSP environment that democratizes complex mathematical sound design. Utilizing highly optimized Web Audio API `AudioWorklet` processing and a Fast Sine Wavetable, it allows users to generate up to 20,000 simultaneous ring-modulation frequency tracks in real-time. 

**Unique Value Proposition (UVP):** *Instant, visual, browser-based extreme sound design without the CPU overhead or financial barrier of traditional modular software.*

## 2. Market Sizing
The global audio software market is expanding rapidly due to the booming creator economy, indie game development, and the accessibility of home music production.

*   **Total Addressable Market (TAM): Global Music Production Software Market.**
    *   *Valuation:* ~$3.5 Billion (Estimated 2026).
    *   *Scope:* DAWs (Ableton, Logic), virtual instruments, and audio plugins.
*   **Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM): Sound Design & Audio Effect Plugins.**
    *   *Valuation:* ~$600 Million.
    *   *Scope:* Creators purchasing specialized FX plugins (e.g., Soundtoys, FabFilter, Kilohearts) for creative manipulation.
*   **Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM): Web-based Audio Creatives & Niche Sound Designers.**
    *   *Valuation:* ~$5 Million - $15 Million.
    *   *Scope:* Indie game developers, experimental electronic musicians, and DSP educators willing to pay for highly specialized, highly accessible tools.

## 3. Target Audience & Personas

### Persona 1: The Sound Designer ("The Texture Creator")
*   **Profile:** Creates audio for indie games, sci-fi films, or sample packs.
*   **Pain Point:** Creating complex, alien, or metallic textures usually requires chaining dozens of heavy VST plugins or manually patching modular hardware. 
*   **AuraModulator Solution:** Instantly loads in the browser. A single click of "Generate Base Hz" and "Start All LFOs" creates massive, evolving textures that can be recorded directly to lossless WAV/FLAC.

### Persona 2: The Experimental Musician ("The Ambient Artist")
*   **Profile:** Electronic musicians focused on drone, ambient, or industrial genres.
*   **Pain Point:** Finding generative tools that don't sound like generic, repeating loops.
*   **AuraModulator Solution:** 600-second LFO sweeps across 20 independent engines provide infinite, non-repeating harmonic phasing and micro-tonal shifts.

### Persona 3: The Academic ("The DSP Educator/Student")
*   **Profile:** University professors or students studying digital signal processing and sound physics.
*   **Pain Point:** Existing visualizers are dry; existing modular software (like Max/MSP) has a massive learning curve.
*   **AuraModulator Solution:** Zero installation. Works on school Chromebooks. The Holographic Oscilloscope visually maps abstract mathematical frequencies to real-time wave speed and color.

## 4. Go-To-Market (GTM) Strategy

### A. Content Marketing (The "Shock" Factor)
AuraModulator is highly visual and auditory. Marketing will be entirely video-led.
*   **Short-Form Video (TikTok/Reels/Shorts):** "How to make a cinematic black hole sound in 10 seconds." Videos will showcase a raw, dry vocal input, followed by a screen recording of hitting "▶ Start All LFOs" to reveal the massive holographic visualizer and immediate audio transformation.
*   **Free Sample Packs:** Generate 100 WAV files of sci-fi drones using the app. Distribute the sample pack for free on Reddit/forums in exchange for email sign-ups, with the call-to-action: *"Generate your own infinite variations with AuraModulator."*

### B. Influencer & Affiliate Outreach
The "browser-based" angle lowers the friction for reviewers. They don't need to download or authorize iLok licenses.
*   **Targets:** YouTube audio creators like *Hainbach* (ambient/experimental), *Venus Theory* (sound design), *Benn Jordan* (audio science), and *Andrew Huang* (music tech).

### C. Community Launch
*   **ProductHunt:** Positioned as "The most powerful mathematical audio DSP engine in a browser."
*   **Reddit Communities:** Post interactive challenges on `r/SoundDesign`, `r/synthesizers`, and `r/WebAudio` (e.g., "I built a 20,000-oscillator ring mod in HTML. Here's how it sounds. Roast it.")
*   **Forums:** Gearspace and KVR Audio "New Product Announcements" boards.
